# Operational Scope and Ethical Boundaries

This repository and its artifacts are governed by principles of legality, proportionality, and role separation.

The author operates exclusively within authorized scopes related to Information Security, focusing on detection, analysis, documentation, and governance. Observation of signals or patterns does not imply authority to intervene, enforce, or investigate beyond explicit legal and institutional mandates.

All activities adhere to applicable laws, governance frameworks, and due-process requirements. No content in this repository should be interpreted as investigative action, enforcement authority, or substitution for competent institutions.
